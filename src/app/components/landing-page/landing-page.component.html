Our Story

Dormate was created by a group of BSIT students at Gordon College. The team consisted of a team adviser, a hustler, a hipster, and a hacker from Team e-Tech. This was created because the team saw a problem finding available dormitories for students and workers in Olongapo City.

About the Team

Our Goal
